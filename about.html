About SkillSwap

SkillSwap is built on a simple idea: knowledge is currency. Here, you don’t pay with money, you pay with your skills.

Whether you’re a coder looking to learn guitar, or a chef wanting to understand 3D design, SkillSwap makes it possible. Connect directly with creators and learners, exchange skills, and grow together.

This platform is a step towards a world where learning is open, community-driven, and beautifully balanced.
